68 /* array of timers dedicated to PWM in and out and capture use
69  *** Note that the clock_freq is set to the source in the clock tree that
70  *** feeds this specific timer. This can differs by Timer!
71  *** In PWM mode the timer's prescaler is set to achieve a counter frequency of 1MHz
72  *** In OneShot mode the timer's prescaler is set to achieve a counter frequency of 8MHz
73  *** Other prescaler rates can be achieved by fore instance by setting the clock_freq = 1Mhz
74  *** the resulting PSC will be one and the timer will count at it's clock frequency.
75  */
