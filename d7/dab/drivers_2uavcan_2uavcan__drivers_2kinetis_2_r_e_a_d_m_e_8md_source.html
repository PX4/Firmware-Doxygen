1 # libuavcan_kinetis
2 Libuavcan platform driver for Kinetis FlexCAN
3 
4 The Directory contains the Kinetis FlexCAN platform driver for Libuavcan on NuttX.
5 
6 Configuation is set by the NuttX board.h for the following:
7 
8 | Driver | board.h |
9 |--------|----------------|
10 | OSCERCLK | BOARD_EXTAL_FREQ |
11 | busclck | BOARD_BUS_FREQ |
12 
13 and the following commandline defines:
14 
15 | Setting | Description |
16 |-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
17 |UAVCAN_KINETIS_NUM_IFACES | - {1..2} Sets the number of CAN interfaces the SW will support |
18 |UAVCAN_KINETIS_TIMER_NUMBER | - {0..3} Sets the Periodic Interrupt Timer (PITn) channel |
19
