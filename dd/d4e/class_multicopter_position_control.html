systemlib::Hysteresis MulticopterPositionControl::_arm_hysteresis {false}
private

Hysteresis that turns true once vehicle is armed for MPC_IDLE_TKO seconds.

A real vehicle requires some time to accelerates the propellers to IDLE speed. To ensure that the propellers reach idle speed before initiating a takeoff, a delay of MPC_IDLE_TKO is added.becomes true once vehicle is armed for MPC_IDLE_TKO seconds
